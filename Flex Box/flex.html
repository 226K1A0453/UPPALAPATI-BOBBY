RAIDER APPS

Rapido is an Indian ride-hailing service primarily known for its bike taxi services. It also offers auto-rickshaw and taxi cab hailing, as well as parcel delivery and third-party logistics services. Founded in 2015 and based in Bangalore, it operates in over 500 cities across India

Ola is an Indian company, founded in 2010 by Bhavish Aggarwal and Ankit Bhati, based in Bengaluru. Initially known as Ola Cabs, it primarily provides app-based ride-hailing services in India. Ola also offers various other services through its app, including parcel delivery with Ola Parcel and food delivery

Uber is a multinational transportation company headquartered in San Francisco, California, providing various services like ride-hailing, courier services, food delivery, and freight transport. It operates in over 70 countries and 15,000 cities worldwide
